Restaurant Automation System :



Parth Khaladkar
May 7 2019


This project was created back in 2017 in the third year of my Undergrad studies.The Project aims to simplify taking and tracking orders, inventory management and employee tracking in an self serving restaurant.
The system had two modules

  • Customer Module:
    Customers can place orders using the intuitive GUI,track their orders,give feedback and make payments.It also has the provision for regular customers to login through customer account and view their past transactions.

  • Admin/Employee Module:
    This allowed for the employees to track orders quicky and verify payments. The manager on the other hand to check out inventory status, reports of daily,monthly, yearly revenues and read feedbacks.

Technologies Used

  • JSP (JavaServer pages)
  • HTML/CSS
  • SQL
  • PL SQL
  • Apache Tomcat
